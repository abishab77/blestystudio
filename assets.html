EQUIPMENTS

At Blesty studio we provide an acoustically
treated room which top notch equipment.
Check out the gear we use below

Rode NT1 Microphone

The Rode NT1 is a studio condenser microphone renowned for its ultra-low noise and natural sound reproduction. With a cardioid polar pattern, it captures vocals and instruments with precision, minimizing ambient noise. Its wide frequency response ensures clarity, making it ideal for detailed recordings in professional or home studios. The robust build quality and included shock mount enhance durability and ease of use.

UAD SC-1 Microphone

The UAD SC-1 is a high-performance microphone designed to deliver exceptional clarity and warmth. It is engineered for professional audio production, offering a smooth frequency response that excels in capturing vocals and acoustic instruments. Paired with Universal Audio’s preamps, it ensures premium sound fidelity. Its sleek design complements its studio-grade performance.

UAD Apollo x8

The UAD Apollo x8 is a high-end audio interface equipped with world-class preamps and onboard DSP processing. It provides pristine sound quality with near-zero latency and supports up to 8 channels for versatile recording needs. Integrated Unison technology lets you emulate iconic preamps and compressors, making it perfect for professionals seeking analog warmth in digital production.

Nord Stage 3 Synth

The Nord Stage 3 is a premium synthesizer combining piano, organ, and synth engines in one versatile unit. With its intuitive interface and seamless sound layering, it’s perfect for live performances and studio work. The weighted keys provide a realistic feel, while the extensive library of presets ensures flexibility across genres.

Launchkey 49 MIDI Controller

The Launchkey 49 is a versatile MIDI controller designed for music producers and performers. With 49 velocity-sensitive keys, it provides dynamic control over virtual instruments and DAWs. It features integrated pads, knobs, and sliders for intuitive hands-on creativity, making it a go-to tool for electronic music production.

Ibanez GRRR 330 Guitar

The Ibanez GRRR 330 is a sleek and versatile electric guitar ideal for a wide range of genres. With its lightweight design and fast neck, it offers excellent playability. Equipped with quality pickups, it delivers a rich tonal palette from clean melodies to heavy riffs. Its durable build ensures reliability for stage and studio use.

Marcus Miller Sire V3 Bass Guitar

The Marcus Miller Sire V3 is a professional-grade bass guitar offering exceptional tone and playability. Its active electronics and precision controls provide rich and dynamic sound, perfect for live and studio sessions. The ergonomic design and smooth fretboard make it comfortable for extended playing sessions.

JBL Nano K4 Speakers

The JBL Nano K4 are compact studio monitors delivering powerful and balanced sound. With a focus on accuracy, they provide a clear representation of your mix. Their small footprint and stylish design make them perfect for studio spaces, ensuring professional-quality sound without compromise.

Yamaha MGP24 Mixer

The Yamaha MGP24 is a professional-grade analog mixer with 24 channels and premium audio quality. Its warm preamps and versatile EQ options make it ideal for live sound and studio applications. The compact design and rugged construction ensure reliability and ease of use in any setup.

DT 990 Pro Headphones

The DT 990 Pro headphones are open-back studio headphones known for their detailed and spacious soundstage. With comfortable ear pads and a lightweight build, they are ideal for long mixing or listening sessions. Their precision makes them a favorite for critical listening and sound design.

Mac Studio Computer

The Mac Studio is a powerful workstation designed for demanding creative tasks. Equipped with advanced processors and ample RAM, it handles audio and video production seamlessly. Its compact yet efficient design makes it the centerpiece of any professional studio setup, ensuring high performance and reliability.

Focusrite Scarlett 2i2

The Scarlett 2i2 is a compact and reliable audio interface ideal for beginners and professionals. With two high-quality mic preamps, it delivers clean audio for recording and playback. Its plug-and-play compatibility and durable design make it perfect for portable setups. The direct monitoring feature ensures seamless real-time audio feedback.

Roland SPD-20X Rhythm Pad

The Roland SPD-20X is a versatile percussion pad with a wide range of built-in sounds and customizable options. Its sensitive pads allow for expressive drumming, while the durable design ensures long-lasting performance. It integrates seamlessly into acoustic and electronic setups, making it ideal for live and studio use.

Canon EOS R Mirrorless Camera with Studio Lights

The Canon EOS R is a high-resolution mirrorless camera perfect for professional video and photography. Paired with studio lighting, it ensures crisp visuals and vibrant colors. Its advanced autofocus and customizable settings make it ideal for capturing studio sessions or promotional content with precision.

Indian Instruments (Harmonium, Dholak, Tabla)

The harmonium, dholak, and tabla bring authentic Indian classical tones to the studio. Each instrument is crafted for rich sound quality, offering a range of expressive possibilities. They are essential for creating traditional Indian music or adding a unique cultural flavor to contemporary compositions.
